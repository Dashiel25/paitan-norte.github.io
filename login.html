Paitan Lake - Libsong Lake

Paitan or Libsong Lake is located between three (3) neighboring northern barangays of Cuyapo namely, Paitan Norte, Paitan Sur, and Nagcuralan. It is the only natural in-land body of water in entire Nueva Ecija streching as wide as 85 hectares where migratory birds make their home during September to February of each year.

Situated in the land-locked province of Nueva Ecija, this lake is the only natural inland body of water in the province. It is located in the heart of Cuyapo and the middle of the plains of this little town. This trail starts at Paitan Sur Elementary School and ends near Paitan Norte Elementary School. It goes around the lake and traverses the Central Luzon Border.
