Historical Population

The population of Paitan Norte grew from 1,193 in 1990 to 1,886 in 2020, an increase of 693 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 0.41%, or an increase of 36 people, from the previous population of 1,850 in 2015.
